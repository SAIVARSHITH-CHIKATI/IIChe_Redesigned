IIChE NITR

Indian Institute Of Chemical Engineers or IIChE is a premier professional student chapter of Chemical Engineering in India. lIChE is recognised by Department of Science and Technology, Government of India as a scientific and research organ--isation, Student members pursuing under-graduate degrees in Chemical Engine- -ering form Student Chapters,under the auspices of the respective RegionalCentres.

OUR VISION

More guest lectures, seminars,short stories, plant visits etc at regular intervals organised by the Chapter. To engage students in research projects and softwareprojects(Matlab, Ansys, Aspen etc...). Academic activities apart,student chapters organise cultural events and sports activities for their members. It's a great opportunity for the building chemical engineers to explore the subject in depth.
